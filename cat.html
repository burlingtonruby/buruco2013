Burlington Ruby Conference 2013

Conference Cats

In January 2013 Vermont was ranked as the #1 state for cat ownership per capita by the American Veterinary Medical Association. In order to show our appreciation for our feline friends, we've decided to invite actual cats to sponsor the Burlington Ruby Conference. Each cat sponsor will earn an exclusive spot as a recognized sponsor on the conference website and videos.

What does it cost?

Middle Class Cat - $50 per cat - Picture and name featured on website - Limited to 25.
Aristocat - $100 - Picture and name featured on website and speaker videos - Limited to 15.

Where does the money go?

25% of the proceeds will be donated to the Humane Society of Chittenden County
The remaining 75% will go toward our Opportunity Scholarship program.

Thank you for your support

This program ended on July 1, 2013. Thanks to everyone who supported the first official Ruby Conference cats! The program was a big success, raising a combined total of over $1K for scholarships and our humane society!
