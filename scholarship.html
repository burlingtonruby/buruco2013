Burlington Ruby Conference 2013

Scholarship Application

The Burlington Ruby Conference is offering a handful of scholarships to those who need assistance obtaining a ticket to the conference. We are offering scholarship opportunities to attendees of the Girl Develop It Intro to Ruby workshop and to those who are underrepresented in the Ruby community or cannot afford a ticket to the conference.

If you are interested in applying for a scholarship ticket, please fill out the form below and do not hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.

We are accepting scholarship applications until Friday, July 12th. We will start announcing recipients on Monday, July 15th.
