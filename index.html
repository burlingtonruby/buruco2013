Not Your Average Ruby Conference

The Burlington Ruby Conference is focused on making sure that everyone has an enjoyable weekend. It is that simple. With the opportunity to meet new people and learn a few things, we want to you to enjoy Vermont as much as we do on a daily basis. We consider ourselves a boutique conference, with only 150 tickets available, our goal is to make sure that everyone loves it enough to want to come back again next year.

About Burlington

Burlington, Vermont is a wonderful city next to the gorgeous Lake Champlain. Burlington has a delectable selection of places to eat. Our favorites include The Farmhouse, American Flatbread and Stone Soup. Besides eating, there is a wonderful bike path that goes along the lake and a weekend Farmer's Market in the center of town at City Hall Park.

Staying in Burlington
  • Courtyard Marriott - We have blocked out rooms at a discounted price until July 1st. To get the discounted rate, please mention the Burlington Ruby Conference.
  • airbnb - A more adventurous way to stay in Burlington.
  • Burlington Hostel - A great hotel alternative located a block from the conference location.
Here are some things you can experience while you are here:
  • Hangout with attendees and speakers at our annual lakeside BBQ
  • Shop at the Saturday Farmers Market for some of the freshest produce available in Vermont
  • Enjoy a majestic sunset walk along the waterfront bike path
  • Drink beers from the highest rated breweries in the world
