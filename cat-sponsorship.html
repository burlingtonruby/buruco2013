Burlington Ruby Cats

In January 2013 Vermont was ranked as the #1 state for cat ownership per capita by the American Veterinary Medical Association. In order to show our appreciation for our feline friends, we've decided to invite actual cats to sponsor the Burlington Ruby Conference. Each cat that sponsors the conference will earn an exclusive spot as a recognized sponsor in the conference videos and website, and as a donor to the Humane Society of Chittenden County.

This is not a joke.

What does it cost?

$125 per cat.
Since we are limiting the # of sponsors to 25, we cannot offer discounts for multiple cats.

Where does the money go?

* $25 will be donated to the Humane Society of Chittenden County
* $100 will go toward the cost of conference video production
